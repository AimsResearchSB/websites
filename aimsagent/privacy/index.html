Privacy Policy

AimsAgent  ·  Advertising Inspection Management Research Sdn Bhd  ·  Last updated: 15 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Advertising Inspection Management Research Sdn Bhd ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with the AimsAgent mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

AimsAgent does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All data created or captured within the App — including photographs, videos, inspection records, and detection results — remains exclusively on your device.

2. Location Data

The App accesses location data from your device. This location data is used for GPS-related functionality within the App. Location data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device camera solely to capture images and video for advertising board inspection and analysis purposes. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to our servers.

4. Webhook Integration (User-Configured)

AimsAgent allows you to configure a webhook URL pointing to a server of your choosing. When a webhook is configured, the App may send inspection data (images, metadata, detection results) directly from your device to your own server. We have no access to, and no control over, the data transmitted through user-configured webhooks. You are solely responsible for the privacy practices of your own server endpoint.

5. Data Storage

All App data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate cloud storage, databases, or analytics services that receive your data.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

AimsAgent is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
